Recent Projects

Here’s one of the recent jobs: Room before and after, with a nice wall color and a built-in wardrobe. Jobs done in this room: -Suspended ceiling and 3 layers of carpet were removed - Walls and ceiling plastered and painted. - Measures were taken to eliminate the floor squeak. - Water-resistant laminate flooring was laid, capable of withstanding 100 hours of immersion in water. - The window frame was insulated, addressing finger-wide gaps around it. - A built-in wardrobe with LED lighting (activated when doors are opened) was installed. The wardrobe is based on the IKEA PAX system. Front panels, doors, and shelves were custom-made. - Doors were restored and painted. - A projector was installed, with wires hidden above the ceiling. - Furniture was assembled, and new lights were installed. - The ceiling was insulated from above.
